About Our Magazine

Studying and practicing medicine isn't the same thing. It was really hard to graduate school, but it is a whole nother story to graduate neurology residency, especially in Syria in the last 10 years. It all turned out great. Nothing beats the smile on your face when you hear the word: Graduated. In Syria, although there have bin a war for a really long time, medicine is very good, and the specialist in every field are at top level. I was lucky to be surrounded by high level teachers in every step of the study, whether at The Syrian Arab Red Crescent Hospital or at Damascus Hospital.
